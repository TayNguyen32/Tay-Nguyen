School Projects

"CHOSEN" custom card game

OVERVIEW: created my own game card game based off characters from the MOBA "League of Legends" while mixing it with core gameplay from various social deduction games. Core elements such as card display art and instruction manual were all vectorized and put together for showcase

Dark Knight Rises Figure - ground book cover

OVERVIEW: I had created a custom book cover based off one of my favorite movies “The Dark Knight Rises”. The objective was to use the some Adobe Creative Suite products in order to create a product that could be seen on shelves while showing that my understanding of the figure - ground concept.

Recycling yoda sticker

OVERVIEW: Creation of this sticker was based on wanting to inspire people to recycle more. I wanted to incorporate a very known figure which turned out to be Yoda from the star wars series. The Yoda figure is using the “force” to put the bottle into the recycling bin.

Cereal box concept design

OVERVIEW: I had created a concep collectors edition of a cereal box. Using the various Adobe Suite Products in order to complete this project. Picking a subject which was 2019’s NFL MVP Lamar Jackson to work around. I wanted to incorporate vectors of the athlete along with a rebranded logo.

Custom game with User Interface

OVERVIEW: I had created a new game within the genre classified as a social deduction game. My game was unique as I wanted to fuse with roles from the popular MOBA “League of Legends”. I had written the instructions and everything that came with a fully functional party game within this app prototype.

The Anchor Community Initiative development design

OVERVIEW: helping the Anchor Community Initiative through the development of visual identity design for their Coordinated Entry System and a set of design guidelines that spells out the use of logo(s), fonts, color, and assets. Included was a foldable flowchart/resource page ready for printing.

GWSS black history month celebration media posts

OVERVIEW: My piece had represented the late great Thurgood Marshall as a civil rights activist and American Lawyer. The vectorized art was made for social media view and was a part of a 3-piece post but I had included pieced it together for a better viewing experience.
